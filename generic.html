Exploratory Sales
Data Analyst Project

Analyzed sales data using SQL and Tableau.

A large computer hardware company that has many local stores learned from its annual sales report that sales has been declining. With the sales director asking all the local stores to send their indiviudal sales reports, it is hard to manually track where the business is failing.

Through the use of SQL and Tableau, I was able to highlight that the decreases in sales was a result of certain quarters seeing a rise in revenue but a fall in profit margins. With the need to increase profit marigins, I helped the computer hardware company analyze which local stores produced the lowest profit margins and which products were selling at the lowest profit margins. This combined data would assist the supplier company make the necessary business changes to see a rise in sales in coming years.
